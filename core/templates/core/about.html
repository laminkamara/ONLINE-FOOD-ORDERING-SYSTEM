About yoni Fast Food

Serving Sierra Leone's favorite meals since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, yoni Fast Food Restaurant has been at the heart of Sierra Leone's culinary scene for over 15 years. What started as a small family kitchen has grown into one of the most beloved fast-food chains in the country.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to serve delicious, high-quality food that brings people together. Every dish we prepare reflects our commitment to authentic flavors, fresh ingredients, and exceptional service.

Today, with multiple branches across Freetown and beyond, we continue to innovate while staying true to our roots. From traditional Sierra Leonean dishes to international favorites, there's something for everyone at yoni.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional dining experiences through delicious food, friendly service, and a welcoming atmosphere. We strive to use only the freshest ingredients and maintain the highest standards of quality and hygiene.

Our Vision

To become the leading fast-food chain in West Africa, known for innovation, quality, and community engagement. We aim to expand our reach while maintaining the personal touch that makes us special.
